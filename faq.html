FAQ

Q: How long have you been in business?
A: We have been supporting our local community with tasty tea since 1991. We can not wait for you to give us a try!

Q: What is the owners name?
A: The owner of Tasty Teas is Eric Lester.

Q: Where are you based out of?
A: Tasty Teas is based out of Portland, OR.

Q: How many different teas do you offer?
A: We currently offer five different flavors of Tasty Teas. Have an idea for a new flavor? Reach out to using information from our Contact Us page. If we use the flavor you recommend we will let you name it!

Q: How can I get in contact with you?
A: If you would like to reach out to us for any reason please use the contact information located on our Contact Us page.

Q: Do you have a return policy?
A: As long the packages are unopened we can provide a full refund. If you have already opened your Tasty Tea we can provide up to five exchanges.
